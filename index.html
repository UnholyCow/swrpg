12/6/2015

BoSS Outage

The Bureau of Ships and Services was sabotaged on the Mid Rim planet Ord Mantell. Star Destroyers were dispatched while the matter was being investigated, all current inhabitants should follow any and all instructions from their planetary governor.

Images captured as the suspects fled the BoSS building are attached to this message. If you have any information regarding this group of individuals that leads to their capture, death, or positive identification, a 30,000 $ reward is being offered.

Anyone being caught abusing the temporary outage of records will be imprisoned for no less than 10 standard Imperial years, their assets siezed, and known friends and family questioned.

This flagrant violation will not go unpunished.

11/1/2015

Terrorists Attack Raxus Prime

The Imperial shipyard located at Raxus Prime was attacked by an extremist group. earlier this week. Intelligence confirms their targets were the orbital docks though local system patrol craft were able to intervene before much damage was done.

Of the many craft used in the attack only two severely damaged CEC starships remained, one YT-1300 and a CR90 corvette. If you have any information that leads to the apprehension of the attackers a 3,000 $ bounty will be awarded.
