Whispers of the Past

Six long years after the struggles of the Alliance to Restore the Republic, the impossible happened. The Galactic Empire fell and a NEW REPUBLIC rose to take its place.

The power vacuum created with the loss of loyal Regional Governors and Moffs let most of the galaxy spiral into chaos as criminal syndicates and warlords seized huge regions of space for themselves.

Out of the blue you receive a request to perform a secretive mission for the Republic. Transport is dispatched for you and only then do you learn its destination, CORUSCANT, former heart of the Empire....
