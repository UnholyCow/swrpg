Difficulty Levels

Difficulty Level Dice Example
Simple - Dead simple tasks.
Easy
Picking a primitive lock, tending to minor cuts and bruises, finding food and shelter on a lush planet, shooting target at close range.
Average
Picking a typical lock, stitching up a small wound, finding food and shelter on a temperate planet, shooting target at medium range or trying to strike an engaged target.
Hard
Picking a complicated lock, setting broken bones or suturing large wounds, finding food and shelter on a rugged planet, shooting target at long range.
Daunting
Picking an exceptionally sophistacated lock, performing surgery or grafting implants, finding food and shelter on a barren planet, shooting target at extreme range.
Formidable
Picking a lock with no comprehiensible mechinism, cloning a new body, finding food and shelter on a planet without breathable atmosphere.

Fire, Acid, and Corrosive Atmospheres

Rating Example
1-2 Campfires, mildly caustic substances such as concentrated industrial cleansers, air filled wish ash and fumes from heavy fire.
3-5 Flammable liquids and flame projectors, dangerous substances such as industrial acids, air contaminated by chemical leaks.
6-9 Welding torches, weaponized acids, atmospheres with substantial parts dangerous gasses such as ammonia.
10+ Lava, blast furnaces, atmospheres completely made of unbreathable and hazardous gasses such as ammonia methane mixes with acid vapors.
